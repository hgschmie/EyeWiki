Includes the body, if there were no search results.

----

!Usage

{{{
<wiki:IfNoSearchResults>
   HTML or JSP tags
</wiki:IfNoSearchResults>
}}}

!Parameters

None.

!Example
{{{
<h1>Results of your search</h1>

<wiki:IfNoSearchResults>
    No results found.
</wiki:IfNoSearchResults>
}}}
